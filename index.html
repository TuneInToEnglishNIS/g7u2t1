'Communication Song'

BEFORE LISTENING

Task 1 :Discuss the following questions.
What means of communication do you use in your life?
What are the advantages and disadvantages of using technology for communication?

LISTENING PART 1

With my friends, I chat and talk
On my cell phone as I walk
I click the letters and SMS.
My friends reply using they press.

On my computer, I write a letter.
No need for a postman, it’s so much better!
Just click on “send”, it’s on its way,
And I know it will arrive the very same day.

I use my cell phone to tell Bess
Where to go to buy a dress.
I SMS my friend Jule,
Who at the moment is in school.
I send an e-mail to cousin Bill
Who is in far away Brazil.

I wave “Hello” to my friend Guy
Who just happens to walk by.
I post a letter to Grandpa Brett
Who doesn’t have a computer yet.

With my friends, we keep in touch
And I tell them I love them very, very much.
